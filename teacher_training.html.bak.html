Inside Flow 25 Hour Teacher Training


Yoga Alliance Certified + 25 HR TRC on insideflow.com

Start your Inside Flow career in this 4-day teacher training. The training covers lessons on the key elements of Inside Flow, including theory, practice, music, teaching, and sequencing. Inside Flow is a trademarked vinyasa flow style founded by Young Ho Kim. It is based on the yogic philosophy SPANDA - the pulsation of life. You will learn the foundational principles of how to teach the Inside Flow yoga style and embody Spanda through this invigorating practice connecting breath, rhythm, and flow.

THE TRAINING WILL FOCUS ON:

  • Establish a rhythm and pace that enhances the Inside Flow practice for your students.
  • Learn how to effectively structure an Inside Flow class that flows seamlessly from start to finish.
  • Design playlists that complement the Inside Flow practice and keep your students engaged and motivated.
  • Connect the Inside Flow sequence to a deeper intention and meaning, creating a transformative experience for your students.
  • Step into your leadership as an Inside Flow practitioner and teacher, inspiring and guiding your students towards their full potential.

PRE-REQUISITES:
To ensure that you are fully prepared for the Inside Flow Teacher Training, we require the following prerequisites:

  • Completion of a certified 200-hour yoga teacher training before enrolling in the program. This will provide you with the foundational knowledge needed to fully engage with the training curriculum.
  • Prior attendance of Inside Flow classes is mandatory, as this will give you a solid understanding of the practice and its unique flow. Attending classes beforehand will help you get the most out of the training.
  • A strong vinyasa practice and a passion for music are recommended for the training, as these elements are essential to the Inside Flow practice.

By meeting these prerequisites, you will be ready to dive into the training and gain a deeper understanding of the practice, its philosophy, and its transformative potential.

CERTIFICATION AND LICENSING:
Upon successfully completing the teacher training and passing the teaching exam, you will be awarded the Inside Flow 20hr certificate. It is important to note that Inside Flow is a registered trademark and a branded style, and as such, those who wish to teach it are required to register on insideflow.com as a Silver Instructor and pay an annual licensing fee of 108€.

Already a registered Inside Flow teacher: Add the 20 TRC hours to your existing teacher profile to progress toward your next level.
