Common Questions

The purpose of yoga is an inward journey. While this practice may start in the physical aspect - the hard work is inside our mind and heart. This practice focuses our attention on our body, mind, and heart. Through deep listening within, we learn to connect to ourselves. While it may start with you - the effects of yoga can create waves of changes in your life for the better.

Inside Flow Yoga combines yoga and music created by Young Ho Kim in Germany. This yoga style is based on the yoga concept of spanda - the pulsation of life. Creating expansion and contractions with our breath, movement, and music. In the end, the sequences of asanas are practiced to the rhythm of a song. Moving from one posture to another, we focus on the movement between the postures, uniting the breath, rhythm, and flow. This magic will connect you to heart and soul. Learn more at insideflow.com.
